
                        GALAXY MAP - SPACE TRADING GAME
                     Grid Layout with 4 Pirate Quadrants
    ============================================================================

                         QUADRANT LAYOUT (centered on 0,0)

              Q01: SILK HAND                Q02: PHANTOM CIRCUIT
              (Top-Left, Easiest)           (Top-Right, Easy-Med)
              Smugglers, prefer bribery     Info brokers, trade intel

                          ┌───────┬───────┐
                          │       │       │
                          │  Q01  │  Q02  │
                          │       │       │
                          ├───@───┼───@───┤  @ = Safe Zone (R0101)
                          │       │       │
                          │  Q03  │  Q04  │
                          │       │       │
                          └───────┴───────┘

              Q03: RUST COLLECTIVE          Q04: VOID REAVERS
              (Bottom-Left, Med-Hard)       (Bottom-Right, Hardest)
              Tanky scavengers, won't flee  Most aggressive, high damage

    ============================================================================

    GRID DIMENSIONS:
    - 12x12 grid per quadrant (R0101 to R1212)
    - 10x10 usable core per quadrant (R0101 to R1010)
    - Dead space on outer galaxy edges only (x > 10 OR y > 10)
    - No dead space between quadrants — live borders at center
    - Total grid: 24x24 across all quadrants
    - Origin (0,0) at center — logic only, not accessible in-game

    ============================================================================

    ADDRESS FORMAT: Q[quadrant]-R[xx][yy]-[system]

    Quadrant:  Q01-Q04
    Region:    R[column][row] — 2-digit X and Y within quadrant grid
    System:    4-digit address with rules:
               1st digit: 1-4  (range 1000-4999)
               2nd digit: even (0, 2, 4, 6, 8)
               3rd digit: odd  (1, 3, 5, 7, 9)
               4th digit: 0-9

    Examples:
      Q01-R0101-1000  = Earth (Quadrant 1, column 01, row 01)
      Q02-R0307-4821  = Quadrant 2, column 03, row 07
      Q04-R0909-2470  = Deep Void Reaver territory, near galaxy edge
      Q03-R1010-1210  = Farthest usable corner of Q03 (distance 20)

    1,000 valid addresses per region
    200 populated systems per region (20% density)

    ============================================================================

    DANGER ZONES (Manhattan distance from origin: |x| + |y|):

      Distance 1-3   = SAFE       Space Force controlled, stable prices
      Distance 4-6   = LOW-MED    Pirate activity begins, mixed systems
      Distance 7-9   = MED-HIGH   Deep pirate territory, fewer hubs
      Distance 10+   = EXTREME    Galaxy edge, strongest enemies, best loot

    ============================================================================

    SAFE ZONE (Space Force Controlled):
    4 regions at center, one per quadrant:
      Q01-R0101 — Earth's region (tutorial + main story start)
      Q02-R0101 — Safe zone, Phantom Circuit quadrant
      Q03-R0101 — Safe zone, Rust Collective quadrant
      Q04-R0101 — Safe zone, Void Reavers quadrant

    Properties: stable/low prices, no pirate activity, full civilization

    ============================================================================

    DEAD SPACE BORDER:
    Rule: dead space exists where x > 10 OR y > 10
    - Outer galaxy edges only — NOT between quadrants
    - Accessible but empty — only dead systems and void
    - Nav computer: "Entering uncharted dead space. No viable systems detected."

    ============================================================================

    JUMP DRIVE TIERS (Manhattan distance from current region):
      Tier 1:  2 regions   — Safe zone + immediate neighbors
      Tier 2:  5 regions   — Nearby quadrant territory
      Tier 3:  9 regions   — Mid-game, most hub regions reachable
      Tier 4: 14 regions   — Deep space, far edges
      Tier 5: Unlimited    — Full galaxy access

    ============================================================================

    PIRATE FACTION TERRITORIES:
    Each faction controls one quadrant. Difficulty scales with quadrant:

      Q01  Silk Hand         Easiest    Smugglers, prefer bribery, flee if outmatched
      Q02  Phantom Circuit   Easy-Med   Info brokers, evasive, trade intel
      Q03  Rust Collective   Med-Hard   Tanky scavengers, want cargo, won't flee
      Q04  Void Reavers      Hardest    Most aggressive, high damage, rarely flee

    FACTION BORDER BLENDING (within 2 regions of quadrant border):
      2 regions away:       75% home faction / 25% neighbor faction
      1 region / on border: 50/50 mix — turf war zones

    ============================================================================

    POPULATION TIERS (per region, weighted by distance):
      Major    (12-18 discoveries)  — common near center, rare at edges
      Moderate (7-11 discoveries)   — common everywhere
      Minor    (3-6 discoveries)    — common at edges
      Measly   (3 discoveries)      — dominant at galaxy edge

    SYSTEM ALIGNMENTS (weighted by distance):
      Friendly          — dominant near center, rare at edges
      Neutral           — even spread
      Hostile           — increases toward edges
      Pirate-Controlled — dominant at galaxy edge
      Dead              — even spread

    ============================================================================

    WORLD GENERATION ORDER:
    1. Generate all systems as Friendly first
    2. Apply transformation rules to convert systems to Neutral, Hostile,
       Pirate-Controlled, and Dead based on distance and regional weights
    3. Clearing a Pirate-Controlled system restores its original Friendly state
